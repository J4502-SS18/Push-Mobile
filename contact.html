Bio

Christopher Guess is a Donald W. Reynold Residential Fellow at RJI at the University of Missouri. He created PUSH while a former ICFJ Knight with The Organized Crime & Corruption Reporting Project (OCCRP). He began his career as a photojournalist, and works freelance as a technologist, disaster specialist as well.
